Method: 对克尔时空进行长时间域模拟，演化ℓ'={0,1,2,3,4}的外向多极源，在无量纲自旋a/M={0.0,0.8,1.0}的背景上，提取ℓ≤4模式的晚期衰减率，使用具有1/r²衰减的非线性启发式外向源。

Result: 在所有情况下，发现源驱动的逆幂律指数pℓℓ'比无源的Price定律值大1个单位，即p_sourced = p_Price + 1。对于(ℓ,m)=(4,4)的类似源驱动引力波情况，也确认了与近期文献密切相关的结果。

Conclusion: 源驱动的标量场尾迹在克尔时空中表现出系统性的衰减行为：衰减指数比无源情况增加1个单位，这一规律在从史瓦西到旋转黑洞的扩展中保持一致性，为理解源驱动扰动在黑洞时空中的演化提供了重要见解。

Abstract: We present the long-duration time-domain simulations of scalar-field tails in Kerr spacetimes driven by
  \emph{outgoing} multipolar sources. Extending the recent work in the literature from Schwarzschild to rotating black holes, we evolve
  sources with $\ell'=\{0,1,2,3,4\}$ on backgrounds with dimensionless spin $a/M=\{0.0, 0.8, 1.0\}$ and extract the late-time decay rates of
  measured modes $\ell\le4$ for a nonlinearity-inspired outgoing source with a $1/r^2$ fall-off. In all cases we find the inverse
  power-law index $p_{\ell\ell'}$ to be larger than the source-free Price law values by one unit, i.e. $p^{\text{sourced}}_{\ell\ell'} =
  p^{\text{Price}}_{\ell\ell'} + 1$. We also include a power-law index value computation for a similar source-driven gravitational wave
  case $(\ell,m)=(4,4)$ and confirm closely related results in the recent literature.
